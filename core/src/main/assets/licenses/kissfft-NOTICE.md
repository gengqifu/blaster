KissFFT notice
==============

MVP-3 builds Chromaprint with its bundled KissFFT source path selected through
`FFT_LIB=kissfft`.

KissFFT upstream source:
https://github.com/mborgerding/kissfft

Chromaprint includes a third-party copy under `src/3rdparty/kissfft`.
The KissFFT copy used by Chromaprint includes `COPYING` and `LICENSES/Unlicense`
files in the upstream source tree.
